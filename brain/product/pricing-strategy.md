# Pricing Strategy

Date: 2026-06-03

## Status
Recommendation, not yet a final pricing decision.

## Recommended Launch Posture
Run afterservice as a free beta for the first validation window, but show future paid plans publicly enough that users understand this is a real business product.

Recommended wording:
- Free during early access.
- No credit card required.
- Beta users keep a founder-rate discount when paid plans launch.

Avoid promising "free forever" for active businesses because follow-up volume, messaging costs, support, imports, and onboarding can become expensive.

## Why Free First
afterservice is still proving a narrow wedge: post-job follow-up for small installers, repair shops, and home-service operators that are not ready for full field-service software. Free beta reduces signup friction and lets the product learn from real customer/job/follow-up data.

## When To Charge
Start charging when at least one of these is true:
- 3 businesses use the board weekly for 3 consecutive weeks.
- 5 businesses import real completed jobs or customers.
- 3 businesses say they would pay $49-$99/month.
- One user asks for a production integration, automation, or team workflow.
- Messaging/provider costs begin to scale with real usage.

## Recommended Paid Plans

### Free Beta
- Price: $0 during early access.
- Users: 1 owner/admin.
- Limits: 100 customers, 200 follow-ups, 5 templates.
- Unlocks: customers, jobs, follow-up board, manual send logging, basic templates.
- Purpose: learn whether operators rely on the workflow.

### Starter
- Price: $29/month, or $290/year.
- Users: 1.
- Limits: 250 customers, 750 follow-ups, 10 templates.
- Unlocks: core board, templates, CSV import, manual email/SMS logging, basic reports.
- Buyer: solo owner/operator.

### Shop
- Price: $79/month, or $790/year.
- Users: up to 5.
- Limits: 2,000 customers, 7,500 follow-ups, 50 templates.
- Unlocks: team assignment, saved follow-up sequences, provider integrations, review-safe request workflow, recurring maintenance reminders, priority import help.
- Buyer: small shop with an office/admin workflow.

### Growth
- Price: $149/month, or $1,490/year.
- Users: up to 15.
- Limits: 10,000 customers, 30,000 follow-ups, 150 templates.
- Unlocks: automation rules, webhook/Zapier import, QuickBooks/job-source helpers, reporting, multi-step campaigns, priority support.
- Buyer: larger local operator with repeat-business or warranty workflows.

## Feature Gating Rule
Do not gate the main value too early. Customers, jobs, the board, and basic templates should remain available in beta and Starter. Gate scale, automation, integrations, team workflows, reporting, and support.

## Pricing Principle
Stay clearly below full field-service platforms. afterservice should feel like a focused post-job tool, not a half-sized Jobber or Housecall Pro.
