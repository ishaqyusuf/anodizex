# Product Vision

## Purpose
This file defines the product direction for Anodizex.

## Vision
Anodizex presents, sells, and manages premium aluminium architectural systems: windows, sliding systems, doors, façades, and related products for residential, commercial, and contractor-led projects.

## Audience
- Homeowners planning aluminium window, sliding door, or entrance upgrades.
- Architects, builders, and contractors comparing aluminium systems.
- Commercial clients evaluating façade, storefront, and entrance solutions.
- Internal sales or operations users managing inquiries, quotations, projects, and customer follow-up.

## Reference Companies
- Schüco: aluminium windows, doors, façades, and sliding systems.
- Reynaers Aluminium: aluminium windows, doors, sliding/folding systems, and façades.
- Kawneer: commercial aluminium windows, entrances, storefronts, and curtain wall systems.
- TECHNAL: aluminium windows, doors, sliders, façades, pergolas, and solar protection.
- AluK: aluminium construction systems, windows, doors, sliding doors, folding doors, curtain walling, shutters, and louvers.
